== Description ==

Add the Clientity Chatbot window to your WordPress site.

Clientity is an automatic chatbot to enhance the customer support and user experience in your website.
It also converts your visits into leads, by obtaining the visitor's contact information.

**Clientity is FREE** and assits your website visitors 24/7.

More info about Clientity at [clientity.com](https://clientity.com/)

Clientity works in **English** and **Spanish**.
[Contact us](https://clientity.com/#contact) to ask about your language.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I get a bot code? =

Sign up free at [clientity.com](https://clientity.com/). Then, sign in and go to **Integrations > WordPress** to see your bot code.


= ¿Dónde obtengo mi código de bot? =

Registrate gratis en [clientity.com](https://clientity.com/) y verás el código de bot en la sección **Integraciones > WordPress**.
